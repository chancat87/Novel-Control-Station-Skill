# Dialogue Comedy

Comedy dialogue should feel faster and cleaner than ordinary dialogue, but still belong to the speaker.

Use:

- short setups
- delayed admission
- selective misunderstanding
- over-literal response
- under-response to a dramatic moment

Check:

- does each speaker sound like themselves
- can the line be shortened
- is the funny beat placed on the strongest last phrase
- is the subtext doing part of the work
