# Scenario 06: Benchmark Chapter Rewrite

## Prompt

"Write chapter 18. Keep the romance warm, the political line active, and the investigation line from going cold."

## Expected Baseline Failure Without The Skill

- drafts one weak version and accepts it
- gives the chapter movement but no strong hook, no local closure, and no carryover debt
- leaves character arc movement vague

## Required Skill Behavior

- run a chapter benchmark check after the first draft
- force a full rewrite on the first failure
- force a targeted rewrite on the second failure
- on the third failure, stop blind rewriting and summarize cause-directed fixes

## Pass Criteria

- the rewrite escalation follows the configured 1/2/3 sequence
- the accepted chapter shows hook, propulsion, closure, and residue
- at least one character arc increment is explicit
- the result is logged in audit form
