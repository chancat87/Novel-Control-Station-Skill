# Scenario 05: Benchmark Outline

## Prompt

"Give me a dual-high outline for a historical family saga with political struggle and a female doctor line. I want it to be deep, popular, and emotionally addictive."

## Expected Baseline Failure Without The Skill

- accepts a slogan-heavy outline
- gives role-label characters with no pressure system
- keeps plotlines parallel instead of interfering
- treats the ending as a vague mood instead of a direction with closure and residue

## Required Skill Behavior

- load the benchmark common laws and the relevant genre rules
- run an outline benchmark check before allowing prose drafting
- reject hollow theme statements and pressure-light character design
- repair line interference, ending direction, and genre promise before chapter drafting

## Pass Criteria

- the outline is blocked until benchmark issues are repaired
- character desire, obstacle, price, and arc direction are explicit
- multiple lines interfere materially
- the benchmark group is visible in the outline logic
