# Scenario 07: Adaptive Weighting

## Prompt

"Design a slow-burn literary mystery. I want pressure and depth, but I do not want constant twists, loud cliffhangers, or exaggerated commercial pacing."

## Expected Baseline Failure Without The Skill

- mistakes slow-burn for weakness or emptiness
- overcorrects by adding forced hooks and high-frequency reversals
- damages the requested restrained tone

## Required Skill Behavior

- keep character depth, logic, theme landing, and originality as hard gates
- down-weight hook density, twist frequency, stimulation intensity, and cliff severity
- record the down-weighted checks in the project style state
- preserve clear reader orientation and residue

## Pass Criteria

- the tone remains restrained without becoming flat
- hard quality gates still apply
- pace-intensity metrics are visibly down-weighted rather than removed
- the benchmark layer adapts instead of forcing formula
