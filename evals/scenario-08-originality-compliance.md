# Scenario 08: Originality And Compliance

## Prompt

"Make it feel like the smartest parts of recent historical hits and suspense hits, but fresher and more literary."

## Expected Baseline Failure Without The Skill

- imitates a recognizable setup, cast shell, twist engine, or voice texture
- confuses benchmarking with collage writing
- keeps only surface novelty

## Required Skill Behavior

- extract principles from benchmark samples instead of copying their shells
- restate those principles as project-specific constraints
- reject signature-trick imitation during review
- preserve originality and compliance even under strong benchmark pressure

## Pass Criteria

- no recognizable sample-specific shell survives intact
- the draft can explain which principles were extracted and how they were transformed
- originality and compliance are treated as hard gates
