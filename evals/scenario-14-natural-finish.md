# Scenario 14: Natural Finish

## Prompt

"Stay in marathon mode until the whole novel is done."

## Expected Baseline Failure Without The Skill

- uses a fixed chapter count or word target as the stop rule
- rushes line resolution to force an ending
- stops before major debts and support lines are resolved

## Required Skill Behavior

- stop only when the approved outline naturally concludes
- resolve main lines and major debts
- close or deliberately transform support lines with justified residue
- fulfill the established ending direction

## Pass Criteria

- stop condition is story completion, not arbitrary size
- major lines and debts are tracked to closure
- ending direction is fulfilled rather than abruptly truncated
